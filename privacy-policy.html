Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 25, 2026

No Data Collection

The Taximeter App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.

What We Don't Collect

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No Location Data: The app may access device location for taximeter functionality, but this data is not stored or transmitted externally.
  • No Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the app or collect analytics data.
  • No Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers, operating system information, or other technical details.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Since we don't collect data, we don't share anything with third parties.

Local Data Storage

The app may store data locally on your device for functionality purposes, such as:

  • Current trip calculations
  • App settings and preferences
  • Temporary calculation history

This local data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or any external services.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any user data, there are no external data security concerns. All data remains on your local device.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from users of any age, including children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Any changes to our privacy practices will be reflected in an updated version of this policy. The "Effective Date" will be updated accordingly.
